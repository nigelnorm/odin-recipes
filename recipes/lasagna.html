Homemade Lasagna

Description

Someone's mom made this recipe. It takes almost 3 hours to make and is 8 servings. Though it may seem like a bit of time, it's not hard at all. Lets dive right into it.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
